Carbohydrate Analysis from Biomass Live Demo

Date: February 26
Time: 2:00 PM GMT
Location: Live online via Microsoft Teams

Join us for a live online demonstration focused on the analysis of carbohydrates from biomass using ion chromatography with amperometric detection.

This session will showcase how the Metrohm 930 Compact IC Flex can be applied to complex biomass and biorefinery samples, delivering selective, sensitive, and reproducible carbohydrate analysis.

The live demo will be hosted by Andrew Osborne (Metrohm UK) and includes application insight from Dr Mohammad Alyassin (University of Huddersfield), alongside short expert contributions from Patrick Klin (Merck) and Bethany Campbell (ELGA Water).

You will see a real-time chromatographic separation of key sugars such as glucose and xylose, with discussion of high-pH operation, pulsed amperometric detection, and data handling using MagIC Net software.

  • Understand why amperometric detection is ideal for carbohydrate analysis
  • See live separation of sugars from complex biomass matrices
  • Learn how reagents and water purity impact IC performance
  • Ask questions directly during the live Q&A session
