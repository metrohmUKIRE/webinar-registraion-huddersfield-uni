Choose between the 930 or 925 IC

The 930 Compact IC Flex is a versatile, robust, and easy-to-use ion chromatograph designed for routine analysis in contract and quality control laboratories across all sectors. It offers superior sensitivity for anions, cations, and polar substances, flexible upgrade options for system expansion, microbore configurations for enhanced efficiency and sensitivity, straightforward automation for faster and more reliable results, integrated inline sample preparation for challenging matrices, a low cost of ownership through reduced consumable use, and secure GLP- and FDA-compliant data management via MagIC Net software.

The 925 IC is an ion chromatography system that focuses on the essentials without compromising on quality, robustness, or reliability. It is ideal for routine water analysis and higher education laboratories, and can also be configured as an automated system to save time and reduce manual workload.

Either IC system can be combined with an 863 Compact IC Autosampler, complete with rack, vials, and caps, allowing up to 36 samples to be analysed fully automatically.When connected to an ultrapure water system such as the PURELAB® flex 3+ Ultrapure Water System, the system is prepared for continuous 24/7 operation.
